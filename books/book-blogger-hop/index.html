Book Blogger Hop #1 (01/06-01/12/2017)

January 6, 2017    book blogger hop


Book Blogger Hop is a weekly meme run by Coffee Addicted Writer. Every Friday, a question is posted for the book blog community to discuss. This week’s question is…

Where’s your favourite place to read? Submitted by Ronyell @ Rabbit Ears Book Blog

I will be honest… I don’t think I have a favourite place, as long as I’m reading. In terms of places where I usually read is on the bus or when I’m eating. I wish I had a better place to read, it’s been a while since I’ve sat down in a dedicated spot for reading at home.
