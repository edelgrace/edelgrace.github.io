Follow Friday #8

Follow Friday is a weekly book meme hosted by Parajunkee and Alison Can Read. In this meme, us bloggers are asked a question to which we respond to by posting on our blogs. Other bloggers can see our answers, comment, and follow!

This week's question is: What’s the most unique character name you’ve come across?

In the spin-off novel of the popular manga and anime series of Death Note, Death Note: Another Note by NISIOSIN, there are a whole load of crazy names. Beyond Birthday, Believe Bridesmaid, Quarter Queen, and Backyard Bottomslash are some of them. They are ridiculous yet unique. The names are mostly plot devices and because it's an English translation, they needed to make up names that would make the story make sense in English. Still doesn't take away from the fact that it's ridiculous.

If we're talking about characters in general, there's this one character from the video game series Assassin's Creed who has the name Altair. It's pronounced like "Al-tie-air" which is pretty cool. There's also a character named Ezio. Cool names if you ask me.
